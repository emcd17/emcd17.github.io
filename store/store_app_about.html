Who We Are

Fun, Fresh Desserts is an Indianapolis based bakery that creates and sells many types of fresh desserts. Our staff are made up of many professional bakers and amateur bakers that are being trained by our pros.
This bakery was started by Jane Doe in 1970 and continues to be a favorite of the Indianapolis community. If you are interested in doing business with us, please stop by our shop at 1234 Fake Street or call us at 123-456-7890 to place your order today!
